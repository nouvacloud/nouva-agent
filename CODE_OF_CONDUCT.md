# Code Of Conduct

Contributors are expected to communicate respectfully, stay technical, and avoid abusive or
discriminatory behavior.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, doxxing, intimidation, and deliberate
disruption of project discussions.

If you experience or observe a problem, contact `contact@nouvacloud.com`. Nouva maintainers may
edit, hide, or remove content and restrict participation when needed to keep the project safe and
productive.
